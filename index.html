Game Mode:

Object

Be the first player to get four of your chekers in a row.

Instructions

Hello and welcome to the game of Connect Four. Each Player will be assigned a checker color (Red or Yellow). First you will need to choose your Game Mode. If you would like to play against an AI, you should click the "1 Player" button. If you would like to play with a friend, click the "2 Player" button. This will start the game and the first player will drop their Red Checker in a column of their choice. Then, Player 2 will drop their Yellow Checker. This will go on until one player gets the bragging rights of getting 4 of their Checkers in a row! Have Fun!
